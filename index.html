About FinanciaLit

With each passing year there’s more agreement that our young people should enter adulthood financially literate. In the face of offshoring, artificial intelligence, automation and a host of other challenges, it wouldn’t seem possible for the stakes to be any higher. Then came the coronavirus. It’s a break the glass moment in America. It’s time to gain mastery of money and finance, and to use it to chart a better course. It’s time to become FinanciaLit.

Author

For the better part of two decades, educator, entrepreneur and author Tom Barrella has been keeping it real as the finance guru at one of America’s top high schools. Each year he and his business education colleagues teach hundreds of students in courses from personal finance to marketing to real estate law. He’s watched with concern as a confluence of factors pushes Americans closer and closer to the financial edge, striving to provide his students with the tools necessary to chart their own courses and build their lives on solid financial foundations.

The Federalist

Besides his teaching and writing credentials, Tom has gone out into new ventures and was recently featured on "The Federalist - Radio Hour", a financial education podcast on the episode "World Famous Econ Teacher Explains How To Be Financially Smarter Than 99% Of Americans".
